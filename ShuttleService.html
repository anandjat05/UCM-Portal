University Of Central Missouri Shuttle Service For Spring 2018

University of Central Missouri offers shuttle service from UCM Campus located in Warrensburg to
UCM Missouri Innovation Campus located in Lee Summit.Students can avail the shuttle service with desired location.

Students To Avail Shuttle Services should have

  • University Student ID
  • Valid Buss Pass
    • Depends on the period to opt for Pass
    • Can be per ride/Semester pass

For Questions:

Please Contact Lee's Summit Shuttle Service Department
(816-347-1612) Or (660-543-8228)

Shuttle Schedule

From Warrensburg Campus To Lee's Summit Campus

The shuttle picks up students from the Multipurpose Building (Bus Stop Lot 42) then picks up students from James C. Kirkpatrick Library (Bus Stop Lot 29).

Scheduled departure from Lot 42, North of the Multipurpose Building at :-

Lot42 Saturday 6.45AM,9.45AM,1PM AND 4PM

Location Days Scheduled Timing
